Cookie Policy

We use cookies and related browser storage to keep the game working, pass limited data to Google services, and support ads. Some of those cookies are first party, and some come from third-party providers like Google.

Game storage

Your guesses are kept in browser memory and/or browser storage so the session can continue while you play. This is for gameplay state, not for user accounts.

Analytics

We use cookies and related identifiers to help send limited information to Google Analytics so we can understand how people are playing the game.

Ads

Google Ads may place third-party cookies or similar technologies to deliver and measure advertising on the site.

If you clear your browser storage or block cookies, parts of the game or its analytics may not behave exactly the same way.
