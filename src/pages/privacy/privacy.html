Privacy Policy

I want to keep this simple: there are no accounts, no profiles, and no epstein files. The only thing we intentionally send out is limited gameplay analytics so I can look at aggregate guess stats and keep the site fun.

What we collect

I do not ask you to sign up, log in, or create an account. I do not sell personal data, and I do not even have a database for this game.

Gameplay analytics

When you make guesses, I send limited event data to Google Analytics, such as guess outcomes, and other broad gameplay metrics. This is only used to understand how the game is being played in aggregate.

What we do not do

I do not build personal player profiles nor do I want you, and I will obviously not use the game to collect your identity, email address, or account history. If you are looking for an account system, it simply does not exist here. If you are law and trying to find something out through the site, there is NOTHING I keep.

By using the site, you agree that very limited analytics may be processed by Google services and that your gameplay session is handled only as needed to run the game.
